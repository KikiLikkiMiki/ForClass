Best Places to Visit in Burgas

Discover the top attractions on Bulgaria's Black Sea Coast

Sea Garden

A huge seaside park perfect for walking, beaches, sculptures, and sunset views.

Burgas Beach

Golden sand beaches located next to the Sea Garden, ideal for swimming and relaxing.

St. Anastasia Island

A historic island with a monastery, museum, and amazing sea views.

Aqua Calidae Thermal Baths

An ancient Roman bath complex known for its mineral springs.

Poda Nature Reserve

A paradise for bird watchers and nature lovers.
